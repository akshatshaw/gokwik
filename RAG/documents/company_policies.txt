# Company Policies and Guidelines

## Work Schedule
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Employees are entitled to a one-hour lunch break and two 15-minute breaks during the day.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week after completing their probation period (90 days). Remote work requires manager approval and must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance.

## Leave Policy
- Annual Leave: 20 days per year
- Sick Leave: 12 days per year
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 4 weeks for secondary caregiver
- Public Holidays: As per local calendar

## Performance Review
Performance reviews are conducted quarterly. Employees receive feedback from their direct manager and peers. Annual bonuses are determined based on individual and company performance.

## Professional Development
Each employee has an annual learning budget of $1,500 for courses, conferences, and certifications. Approval required from manager before enrollment.

## Code of Conduct
All employees must maintain professional behavior, respect diversity, and adhere to our values of integrity, innovation, and collaboration.
