# Product Information Document

## Product Overview
Our flagship product, TechWidget Pro, is an advanced automation tool designed for enterprise businesses. It was launched in Q2 2023 and has served over 500 companies worldwide.

## Key Features
- Real-time data synchronization across multiple platforms
- Advanced AI-powered analytics dashboard
- Customizable workflow automation
- 99.9% uptime guarantee with 24/7 support
- Enterprise-grade security with SOC 2 compliance

## Pricing
- Basic Plan: $99/month for up to 10 users
- Professional Plan: $299/month for up to 50 users
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing for 50+ users with dedicated support

## Technical Requirements
- Minimum 8GB RAM
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection with at least 10 Mbps
- Compatible with Windows 10+, macOS 11+, and Linux
